Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started

How do I purchase artwork?

Browse our collections, select your favorite pieces, and add them to your cart. You can also book a curator consultation for personalized recommendations.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide with insured, climate-controlled packaging. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Can I return or exchange artwork?

We offer a 14-day return policy for most items. Original artworks may have different terms. Contact us for details.

How do I become a featured artist?

We're always looking for talented artists! Submit your portfolio through our contact form selecting "Artist Collaboration" as the inquiry type.
